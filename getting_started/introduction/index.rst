Introduction
============

This series will introduce you to Rebel Toolbox and give you an overview of its
features.

In the following pages, you will get answers to questions such as "Is Rebel Toolbox for
me?" or "What can I do with Rebel Toolbox?". We will then introduce the Rebel Engine's most
essential concepts, run you through the Rebel Editor interface, and give you tips to
make the most of your time learning it.
